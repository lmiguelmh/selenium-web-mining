IRS is seeking tax evaders who use bitcoin
Bitcoin is an online currency that allows people to make one-to-one transactions, buy goods and services and exchange money across borders without involving banks, credit card issuers or other third parties.
The tax agency sent a broad request Thursday to Coinbase, the largest bitcoin exchange in the United States, asking for the records of all customers who bought virtual currency from the company from 2013-15.
